Thijmen Francken

With a BA in Business Administration and an MA in Entrepreneurship, Thijmen helped the startup accelerator Startupbootcamp build a new venture named The Talent Institute (TTI) to teach people product development and online marketing skills. Besides his full-time job at the institute, Thijmen supported many companies with their online marketing as a freelancer. After leading TTI as a managing director towards sustainable revenues, Thijmen joined the venture capital fund Venturebuilders to help grow the scalable, technology-driven startups in their portfolio. He was responsible for online marketing efforts like SEA, SEO, Social, Customer journey tracking & automation, Experimenting, Push notifications, Email automation. His focus is on growth through data-driven marketing.
